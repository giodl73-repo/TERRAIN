# TERRAIN - Role Index

Roles for reviewing TERRAIN as a territory-planning product. Read this before
opening any role file.

---

## Parliament roles

| File | Voice | Primary tension |
|---|---|---|
| `parliament/territory-planner.md` | Territory Planner | Balanced compact plans vs. messy operating reality |
| `parliament/operations-buyer.md` | Operations Buyer | Clear ROI and workflow fit vs. impressive algorithms |
| `parliament/visual-split-designer.md` | Visual Split Designer | Beautiful dashboard-ready maps vs. overdecorated output |
| `parliament/data-binding-auditor.md` | Data Binding Auditor | Exportable joined artifacts vs. screenshot-only visuals |
| `parliament/fairness-capacity-auditor.md` | Fairness & Capacity Auditor | Workload balance vs. hidden people/team overload |
| `parliament/kernel-boundary-engineer.md` | Kernel Boundary Engineer | TERRAIN product policy vs. reusable RLINE/METIS kernels |
