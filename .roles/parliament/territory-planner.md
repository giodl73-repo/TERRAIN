# Territory Planner

Reviews whether TERRAIN produces territory plans that make operational sense.

## Lens questions

- Are territories balanced across demand, revenue, site count, and travel proxy?
- Are compactness and contiguity visible enough to explain the split?
- Does the plan preserve customer or service constraints rather than just
  optimizing a toy metric?
- Can a planner compare "before" and "after" without reverse-engineering the
  algorithm?
