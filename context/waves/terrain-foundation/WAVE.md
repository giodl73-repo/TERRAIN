# Wave: TERRAIN Foundation

## Goal

Create the first TERRAIN workspace and prove a minimal balance-audit contract
for territory plans.

## Thesis

The first useful artifact is not a map; it is an auditable territory plan that
shows whether demand and revenue are balanced enough to be worth operational
review. Mapping and partition refinement can land after this contract is stable.
